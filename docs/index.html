Welcome to Project Spyglass!

Spyglass is a 3-week, augmented reality (AR) proof of concept (POC) by Valtech and Contentstack.

The Spyglass POC uses AR to help demystify skincare products in a retail and home setting. We explain much more in the behind-the-scenes blogposts.

This repository is very much a proof of concept. You may find some things still do not work as you might as expect. Right now, the demo has three scenarios, but they are not seamlessly connected to each other.

Try it now:

  1. Download and print this sheet of bottle labels.
  2. Cut out the labels and wrap them around small bottles in your home. (We used 30mL eyedropper bottles.)
  3. Open the demo on your phone or tablet:
    https://spyglass.valtech.engineering/skincare/
  4. Hold the bottle in front of your phone's camera and rotate it.
